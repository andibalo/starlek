CITY OF STARS

Welcome to Starlight City, a city full of excitement! But suddenly, the global pandemic struck and the city was no longer the same. The Covid-19 pandemic hits really hard that it disturbs the mental health of the Starlight City population. People are anxious, empty, angry, depressed, and even scared to get out. To help the people deal with this fear, Starlight arranges another annual event to lighten back the city by only expressing talents and creativity. So, are you ready to join the fun on the quest to find the next rising star?

INFINITE

In 2021, it will be the 8th edition of Starlight UMN. The number 8 has a special meaning which resembles the symbol of infinity. The theme INFINITE was raised to illustrate the ability to explore self potential as freely as possible like a star that shines without limits.
